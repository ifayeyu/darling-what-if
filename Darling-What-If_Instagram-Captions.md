# Darling, What If — Instagram Captions
### The last 12 posts pushed live · ready to copy-paste

**How to use this file (strategist notes):**

- **The first line is everything.** Instagram truncates captions after ~125 characters, so the hook has to earn the "…more" tap on its own. Every caption below is built hook-first.
- **Optimize for _saves and shares_, not likes.** In 2026 the algorithm ranks reach on saves, shares, and watch-through. Each caption ends with a reason to save or send it to someone.
- **Link:** Instagram kills outbound reach on captions with links. Put the post link in your **bio / Linktree** and say "link in bio." Update the bio link to whichever post you're promoting that day.
- **Hashtags:** drop them in the **first comment**, not the caption, to keep the caption clean. 8–12 is the sweet spot — I've mixed niche + mid-size tags so you're not drowning in giant ones.
- **Format** is suggested per post (Reel = reach, Carousel = saves, Single = aesthetic). Reels get the most new-audience reach right now — lead with them when you can.
- **Voice** is yours: warm, tender, a little literary. Tighten or lengthen to taste.
