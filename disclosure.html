Affiliate Disclosure

In accordance with FTC guidelines, please be aware that some links on Darling, What If are affiliate links. This means that if you click a link and make a purchase, I may receive a small commission — at absolutely no extra cost to you.

"I only ever recommend products, places, and things I genuinely use, love, or believe in. A commission never changes my opinion — my honest voice is the only thing this blog is built on."

Affiliate links may appear in blog posts, resource lists, and recommendations. They are not always explicitly labelled in the body of a post, but this page serves as a standing disclosure for the entire site. If you ever have a question about a specific link, feel free to reach out.

Clicking affiliate links is entirely optional and never required to enjoy any content on this site.

Advertising & Sponsored Content

From time to time, Darling, What If may feature sponsored posts, brand partnerships, or gifted products. When this is the case, the content will be clearly identified as sponsored, gifted, or in partnership with a brand.

  • Sponsored posts — written content created in collaboration with a brand, for which compensation was received
  • Gifted products — items sent by a brand at no cost in exchange for potential coverage

Regardless of whether content is sponsored or gifted, all opinions expressed on this blog are entirely my own. I do not publish sponsored content for products or brands I do not personally support.
